Phones come in all shapes in sizes.
When we think of a phone in 2023 its probably a smart one. However, the history of phones is just as expansive as the access its gives to each individual that has one.

A smart phone can feel like a divination stick in one's hands. The user wave the device around for a signal from beyond, hoping to be showered with strong reception. Yet this divination stick is made of industrial materials glass, plastics, and metal. Where are these materials born? Where do they go to die?
