Time Punch Clock

Documenting labor, perhaps efficiency, maybe a 5 min lie... or the truth. Perhaps logging the hours you could have spent doing something else, but you need money to buy the freerange eggs, right?

Made of leather, metal, and steel - depending on the manufacturer, the materials can be sourced and recycled using waste from other products or can be sourced directly from the original natural resource. Iron and steel is separated from the e-waste, then aluminum, copper and circuit boards are separated. After the bulk metal is removed, the e-waste stream, which is now mainly plastic, is further separated into ABS from polystyrene plastic.

This technology is a surveillance tool catalyzed by the industrial revolution. For optimal use, combine this device with other technologies made of the same materials, such as:

  • a padded aluminum water bottle for hydrating during your grind
  • a wrist watch for quanifying your labor
  • a hi-tec ergonomic chair that prevents you from toppling over at your desk
