Espresso Machine

Espresso is the purest distillation of the coffee bean, the literal essence of a bean. It is a preparation method in which highly-pressurized hot water is forced over coffee grounds to produce a very concentrated coffee drink. The invention of the espresso machine made this accessible to the masses and has since dominated humans' morning routines.

Most auomatic drip coffee maker parts are made of plastic, including the body and basket which hold the filter . The base plate warmer, and heating unit are made out of various metals, usually steel or anodized aluminium. The carafe is made out of heat proof glass.
